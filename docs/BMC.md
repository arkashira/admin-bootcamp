# Business Model Canvas – **admin‑bootcamp**

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • A **structured, beginner‑friendly learning platform** for system administration (Linux, Windows, cloud, networking). <br>• Curated, hands‑on labs with auto‑graded exercises and instant feedback. <br>• Clear learning paths that map to industry certifications (CompTIA Linux+, RHCSA, AWS SysOps). <br>• Integrated sandbox environments (Docker‑based or VM‑based) that require **no local setup**. <br>• Community‑driven Q&A and mentorship, powered by Axentx’s LLM‑assisted tutoring engine. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Individuals** – career‑switchers, students, hobbyists seeking a guided entry into sysadmin. <br>2. **Enterprises & Training Teams** – up‑skilling IT staff, onboarding new hires, compliance training. <br>3. **Educational Institutions** – bootcamps, community colleges, coding schools that need a ready‑made curriculum. |
| **Channels** | • **Web SaaS portal** (admin‑bootcamp.axentx.com). <br>• **Marketplace listings** (AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, Google Cloud Marketplace). <br>• **Partner integrations** – LMS (Canvas, Moodle), HR platforms (Workday, BambooHR). <br>• **Developer community** – GitHub repo, Discord, Reddit, newsletters. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription SaaS** – monthly/annual plans (Individual $19/mo, Team $99/mo per 5 users). <br>2. **Enterprise licensing** – volume‑based annual contracts with SSO, custom branding, analytics. <br>3. **Certification prep bundles** – paid add‑ons for exam vouchers & practice exams. <br>4. **Marketplace revenue share** – 15 % of sales through cloud marketplaces. |
| **Cost Structure** | • **Infrastructure** – compute for sandbox containers/VMs (AWS/EKS, GCP GKE), storage, CDN. <br>• **Content creation & curation** – subject‑matter experts, video production, lab authoring. <br>• **LLM‑assisted tutoring** – licensing for vLLM / SGLang inference, GPU costs. <br>• **Sales & Marketing** – partner commissions, ads, community events. <br>• **Compliance & Security** – SOC2, GDPR, data retention. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Learning content library** (videos, labs, quizzes) stored in the Axentx BRAIN. <br>• **Sandbox orchestration engine** built on vLLM + Docker/KVM for on‑demand environments. <br>• **LLM tutoring layer** (SGLang) for instant hints & code review. <br>• **Customer data & analytics** (progress tracking, skill maps). <br>• **Team** – product lead, curriculum designers, dev‑ops engineers, QA, community managers. |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Curriculum design & updates** – align with latest OS releases & cloud services. <br>2. **Platform development** – maintain sandbox scaling, UI/UX, API for partners. <br>3. **LLM integration** – fine‑tune models on admin‑bootcamp data (auto‑resp, query‑resp datasets). <br>4. **Community engagement** – webinars, hack‑athons, mentorship matching. <br>5. **Compliance & security audits** – ensure sandbox isolation and data privacy. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Cloud providers** (AWS, Azure, GCP) – for sandbox compute credits & marketplace distribution. <br>• **Certification bodies** (CompTIA, Red Hat, Linux Foundation) – co‑branding & exam voucher deals. <br>• **Open‑source projects** – iceoryx2 (IPC labs), vLLM, SGLang for inference infrastructure. <br>• **Educational platforms** – Coursera, Udacity, local bootcamps for curriculum licensing. |
| **Customer Relationships** | • **Self‑service portal** with progress dashboards. <br>• **AI‑driven tutoring** for 24/7 assistance. <br>• **Dedicated account managers** for enterprise accounts. <br>• **Community forums & mentorship program** to foster peer support. |
