# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Admin Bootcamp: Your structured gateway to mastering system administration with beginner-friendly resources and a clear learning path."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Unlock your potential in system administration with Admin Bootcamp's guided learning experience."
2. "Transform your career with Admin Bootcamp: the essential platform for aspiring system administrators."
3. "Navigate the world of system administration effortlessly with Admin Bootcamp's curated learning journey."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Aspiring SysAdmin**  
   - **Daily Job:** College student pursuing a degree in Computer Science, looking to specialize in system administration.  
   - **Budget:** $200/month for educational resources and courses.

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Career Changer**  
   - **Daily Job:** Marketing professional transitioning into IT, seeking foundational knowledge in system administration.  
   - **Budget:** $150/month for online courses and certifications.

3. **Persona 3: Taylor the IT Intern**  
   - **Daily Job:** Intern at a tech company, tasked with supporting system administrators and eager to learn the ropes.  
   - **Budget:** $100/month for supplementary learning materials.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 per acquired customer through organic traffic and lead magnets.

2. **Social Media Advertising (LinkedIn & Facebook)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 per acquired customer through targeted ads aimed at students and professionals in tech.

3. **Partnerships with Educational Institutions**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 per acquired customer through referral programs and bundled offerings.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog series on "Introduction to System Administration" (3 posts).
- **Week 2:** Create and share infographics on "Top Skills for System Administrators" on social media.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar titled "Getting Started with System Administration" and promote it across channels.
- **Week 4:** Release a case study on successful transitions into sysadmin roles using Admin Bootcamp.
- **Week 5:** Publish a guide on "Choosing the Right Tools for System Administration."
- **Week 6:** Share testimonials and success stories from early users.
- **Week 7:** Create video content showcasing platform features and benefits.
- **Week 8:** Launch a newsletter highlighting new resources and upcoming events.
- **Week 9:** Collaborate with influencers in the tech education space for guest posts.
- **Week 10:** Conduct a survey to gather user feedback and iterate on content strategy.
- **Week 11:** Release a comprehensive eBook on "The Path to Becoming a System Administrator."
- **Week 12:** Recap and promote the best content from the previous weeks.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and user interface; begin beta testing with select users.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Admin Bootcamp; initiate marketing campaigns across all channels.
- **D+30:** Analyze user feedback; implement necessary adjustments; host a launch event/webinar.
- **D+90:** Evaluate marketing performance; optimize channels based on CAC; prepare for next phase of growth.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 500 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target $10,000 in MRR by D+90 through subscription sales.
