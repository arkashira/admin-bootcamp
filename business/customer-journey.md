# Customer Journey Map for Admin Bootcamp

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event:** Students realize the need for structured learning in system administration, often after struggling with self-study or informal resources.
- **Friction Points:** Lack of awareness about available structured programs; confusion over where to start; overwhelming amount of information online.
- **User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, and a desire for clarity.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Create engaging marketing content that highlights success stories of past students; offer free introductory webinars or workshops to showcase the platform.
- **Metric:** Number of website visits and engagement rates on marketing content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event:** Students actively search for learning platforms and compare options.
- **Friction Points:** Difficulty in comparing features, pricing, and course content; skepticism about the effectiveness of online learning.
- **User Emotions:** Hopeful yet cautious; feeling overwhelmed by choices.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Provide clear comparisons with competitors; offer testimonials and case studies; create a detailed FAQ section addressing common concerns.
- **Metric:** Number of inquiries or demo requests.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event:** Students sign up for a free trial or demo of the platform.
- **Friction Points:** Technical issues during sign-up; unclear instructions on how to navigate the platform; initial content may not meet expectations.
- **User Emotions:** Excitement mixed with anxiety about the learning experience.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Smooth onboarding process with guided tutorials; personalized support during the trial period; gamification elements to enhance engagement.
- **Metric:** Trial conversion rate (from trial to paid subscription).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event:** Students decide to purchase a subscription after the trial.
- **Friction Points:** Concerns about ongoing value; potential financial commitment; balancing learning with other responsibilities.
- **User Emotions:** Relief and satisfaction, but also apprehension about long-term commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Offer flexible payment plans; provide a personalized learning path based on user goals; regular check-ins to ensure satisfaction and progress.
- **Metric:** Subscription retention rate.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event:** Students complete initial courses and seek further learning opportunities.
- **Friction Points:** Limited awareness of advanced courses or additional resources; potential plateau in learning.
- **User Emotions:** Accomplishment but also a desire for more challenges.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Introduce advanced courses and certifications; create community forums for peer support; offer referral bonuses for bringing in new students.
- **Metric:** Course completion rates and upsell conversion rates for advanced courses.
